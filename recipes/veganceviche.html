Vegan Ceviche

Description

This vegan ceviche is a very easy recipe that can be whipped up in minutes! Serve it on tostadas or chips!

Ingredients

  • hearts of palm (comes in a can)
  • red onion
  • cucumber
  • tomato
  • jalapeno
  • cilantro
  • lime juice
  • olive oil
  • salt and spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the onions in lime juice (to remove bitterness)
  2. Drain, strain and chop the hearts of palm. Rinse.
  3. Chop the cucumbers, tomatoes and jalapeno. Add these to the soaking onions
  4. Chop the cilantro and add it to the bowl along with salt and spices. Give it a good mix.
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or up to 2 hours to let the flavors meld.
  6. Once chilled, taste and adjust salt, spice level and lime juice. I usually add more! For extra richness and drizzle of olive oil is nice too. Right before serving, add avocado!
  7. Serve and enjoy!
